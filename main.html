About Us

The library is a vital resource for research and learning. We provide access to a wide range of books and materials.
The Library collection consists of nearly 4750+ books in various disciplines. These are arranged subject-wise. It has also acquired a few ISO standards (International Standards Organization) in digital form. At present, the main motto is to build up the Library collection such that it has information resources up to Ph.D. level, including advanced texts and reference materials. Our Institute is a Life Member of the prestigious “Current Science Association” of the “Indian Academy of Sciences”, and receiving the “Current Science” journal. It also receives some national newspapers and light reading magazines. Very soon, some popular S&T magazines will be at readers’ disposal, which will make young minds aware of recent happenings in the scientific world. Being a part of Inflibnet -eShodhSindhu, the serious readers can access nearly 7000+ reputed E-Journals (Cambridge, Oxford University, Springer, T&F, etc.), Elsevier ScienceDirect 05 Subject Collection, Databases (MathSciNet, ACM Digital Library, IEEE Electronic Library, JSTOR, Project Muse, etc.) and Society Publications (ACS, AIP, APS, ASME, OSA, ACM, etc.) literature retrospectively. This collection is the bread-n-butter for the Institute’s research scholars and faculty members to quench their thirst for state-of-the-art knowledge.

Rules & Regulations

(Please follow our rules to ensure a pleasant environment for all users.)

General Rules & Regulations of the Central Library


1. Entry only against IITDh ID card.
2. Strict silence is to be maintained in the library.
3. Only notebooks/scribbling pads, pens, pencils, calculators are allowed in the library.
4. Backpack should be deposited outside the library area in the designated place.
5. Use of mobiles and discussions are prohibited in the library.
6. Writing on tables and in books is prohibited.
7. Edibles are not allowed.
8. Waste papers should be put in the dust bins.
9. No library resources are allowed to be taken out of the library without proper authorization.
10. Books should be left on tables after their use.
